Questions agents ask

Virtual staging, answered.

How much does virtual staging cost for agents?

$45 per photo, with volume discounts up to 20%. A typical eight-photo listing runs about $360. No subscription and no minimum, and furniture removal for occupied rooms is available at $55 per photo.

Can I use virtually staged photos on the MLS?

Yes. Every image is built to MLS specifications and AB 723 disclosure standards. We recommend disclosing that photos were virtually staged, and we tell you exactly what to note. Check your local real estate board for any additional requirements.

How fast will my staged photos be ready?

24 to 48 hours from the moment you upload, with unlimited revisions for two weeks and a rework-or-refund guarantee.

Is this AI staging?

No. Every Bella image is composed by hand by a professional interior designer. We don't use AI staging or templates, because they misrepresent properties and produce the same room on every listing in your market.

Do you stage occupied or cluttered listings?

Yes. Furniture removal is included on removal orders, so we digitally clear tenant-occupied, cluttered, or mid-renovation rooms before staging them.
