Questions builders ask

3D rendering & floor plans, answered.

Can you create renderings from floor plans or blueprints?

Yes. We build photoreal 3D exterior and interior renderings directly from your architectural drawings, floor plans, elevations, or CAD files. No finished home or site is required, and that is what makes pre-construction sales possible.

How much does 3D architectural rendering cost?

Residential interior renders start at $630 and exteriors at $840 per view; townhouse, streetscape, and multi-building scenes are priced by scope. Commercial rendering starts at $490. Furnished 3D floor plans start at $84 and 2D floor plans at $24.

How fast are renderings and floor plans delivered?

Most renders and floor plans are delivered in a few business days, with milestone reviews so you approve the camera angle and lighting before we go final. Model-unit virtual staging is delivered in 24 to 48 hours.

Do you handle whole communities and developments?

Yes. We produce streetscape, aerial, and multi-building renderings for master-planned communities, plus per-model interior packages and model-unit staging at volume rates, with a dedicated designer across the project.

Do you stage model units and spec homes?

Yes. We virtually stage model units, spec homes, and empty inventory so your sales office can market finished-looking interiors before furniture is installed, using furniture chosen for your target buyer.
