Questions photographers ask

White-label editing, answered.

Is the staging really white-label?

Yes. Every file comes back with no Bella branding or watermark. You deliver it to your client under your own studio name, and your client never has to know a third party was involved.

What can you do to my photos?

Virtual staging by real interior designers, furniture and clutter removal, day-to-dusk twilight edits, sky replacement, and general photo enhancement. You shoot the room, we return it finished.

How does pricing work for photographers?

Per-photo pricing with volume discounts that scale up to 20% per order. Virtual staging starts at $45 an image and day-to-dusk edits at $7 a photo. The more you send, the less each image costs.

How fast is turnaround?

Standard turnaround is 24 to 48 hours per order, so you can send a shoot and still deliver on your own timeline. Every order includes unlimited revisions for two weeks and a rework-or-refund guarantee.
