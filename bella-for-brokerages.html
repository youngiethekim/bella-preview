Questions teams ask

Volume staging, answered.

Do you offer volume pricing for brokerages?

Yes. Standard volume discounts scale up to 20% per order, and brokerages and teams doing consistent volume qualify for account pricing beyond that. Contact us to set up a brokerage account and we'll scope a rate to your listing volume.

Can every agent on our team order under one account?

Yes. Your agents each submit their own listings while orders roll up to one brokerage account, so you get consistent pricing, one point of contact, and simplified billing across the team.

How do you keep staging consistent across agents?

Every image is composed by a real interior designer to the same standard, and we can align styling to your brand and market so listings look like they came from one brokerage, not twenty different vendors.

How fast is delivery at volume?

Standard turnaround is 24 to 48 hours per order, and it holds at volume. Every order includes unlimited revisions for two weeks and a rework-or-refund guarantee.
